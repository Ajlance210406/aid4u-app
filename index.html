Welcome to Aid4U - Your Emergency Safety Companion

Aid4U is a personal safety app designed to help you in emergencies by sending alerts with your location, providing real-time updates, and much more. Stay safe and connected with your loved ones.

Download Aid4U Now

Features of Aid4U

  • SOS Alert Button: Instantly send emergency alerts to your contacts with your location.
  • Real-Time Location Tracking: Share your location for immediate assistance.
  • Educational Safety Resources: Access tips and guides to stay prepared.
  • Built-in Audible Alarm: Alert people nearby in case of emergency.
  • Offline Emergency Communication: Communicate even when offline.
  • Safety Check-in System: Regular check-ins for peace of mind.

About Aid4U

Aid4U was developed with the vision of empowering individuals to take control of their personal safety. Designed by the 4th year college students of Concepcion Holy Cross College Incorporated in emergency response and technology, Aid4U provides critical safety features for quick actions during emergencies.

Our mission is to save lives by providing reliable tools and educational resources to communities worldwide.
